Getting started!

The app is divided into two main parts. The employees section where you can interact with information on all the currently employeed staff, or the stores section where you can view your store loctations and there products.

Employees guide:

The employees will usually take a few seconds to load after the page, to delete a employees click on the red button near there details, to update there information fill in the box under there details and click the orange edit button, if the changes are not imidiate try refresh the page and the employee details should be up to date.

Stores guide:

The stores will automatically be displayed on the map once the page is loaded, scroll down to see te products currently displayed in each store, click the blue buttons to increase or decrease the quantity of products. Click on a marker to see whos currently works there. click on a empty part of the map to add a new marker.
