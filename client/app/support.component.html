FAQ:

No employees load when I go to the employees page?

Try restarting Mongodb or re-opening the app.

No stores load when I go to the stores page?

Try restarting Mongodb or re-opening the app.

Its says CANNOT GET when I tried load a page?

Try returning to the home page URL or retarting the app.

it says INTERNAL SERVER ERROR when I try open the app?

Make sure Mongodb is running correctly and allowing conenctions.

Its says EAR:3000 is busy?

Make sure angular is done compiling and no other apps are using port 3000.

Google maps remains blank after page is loaded?

Make sure you have an internet connection.

Google map controls for stores page:

  • Click anywhere on the map and enter name of new store to insert a new store.
  • Hold the left mouse button and drag to move around the map.
  • Put two fingers on the touch pad and move the away from each other to zoom out and towards each other to zoom in.
